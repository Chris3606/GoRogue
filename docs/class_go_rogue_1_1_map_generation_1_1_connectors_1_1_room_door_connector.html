Parameters
mapThe map to modify.
roomsA collection of rooms to process.
minSidesToConnectMinimum sides of the room to process. Defaults to 1.
maxSidesToConnectMaximum sides of the room to process. Defaults to 4.
cancelSideConnectionSelectChanceA chance out of 100 to cancel selecting sides to process (per room). Defaults to 50.
cancelConnectionPlacementChanceA chance out of 100 to cancel placing a door on a side after one has been placed (per room). Defaults to 70.
cancelConnectionPlacementChanceIncreaseIncrease the cancelConnectionPlacementChance value by this amount each time a door is placed (per room). Defaults to 10.
Returns
