Creates a tunnel by simply setting as walkable a direct line between the two points specified. In the case of MANHATTAN distance, this is a line as calculated by CardinalPositionOnLine. In the case of EUCLIDIAN or CHEBYSHEV distance, this is a line as calculated by PositionsOnLine (brensham's). The tunnel will be 2-wide horizontally in most cases (this will likely be optional later).

Parameters
mapMap to create tunnel on.
distanceCalcDistance calculation to use – determines whether the tunnel respects 4-way or 8-way movement restrictions.
startStart point of tunnel.
endEnd point of tunnel.
